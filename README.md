# Superstore-Sales-Dashboard-Tableau-Project-
This Tableau project analyzes sales performance, product-level trends, and shipping efficiency across categories, segments, and U.S. states using the Superstore dataset. It features interactive dashboards and actionable business recommendations for optimizing operations and sales strategies.

---

## Executive Summary

This Tableau project explores Superstore sales performance from 2021 to 2024. The dashboard analyzes:

- **Total sales by product and category**
- **Monthly and yearly trends**
- **State-wise delivery lead times**
- **Top-selling items and seasonal patterns**
- **Category-wise contribution to revenue**

It is designed to help business stakeholders identify key sales drivers and regional bottlenecks to improve operations and marketing strategies.

---

## Business Problem

Retail managers and marketing teams need to:

- Understand which products drive the most revenue
- Identify seasonal sales patterns
- Optimize shipping strategy across states
- Improve decision-making using visual insights

---
